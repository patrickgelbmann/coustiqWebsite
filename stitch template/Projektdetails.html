Engineering the Unseen

The Philharmonie Forum presented a unique paradox: how to deliver crystalline orchestral clarity in a space dominated by floor-to-ceiling glass and steel. Sonic Architecture was commissioned to resolve the inherent reverberation challenges without compromising the architect's vision of transparency.

Our solution involved a proprietary **Kinetic Diffusion Array**—hidden within the ceiling structure—that adjusts its angle based on the frequency of the performance. By utilizing micro-perforated acoustic treatments that are visually indistinguishable from solid surfaces, we achieved a "Dry-Wet Balance" typically only found in traditional wooden concert halls.
